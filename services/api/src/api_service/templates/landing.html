How it works

Four steps from upload to results.

1

Submit

POST your document to /v1/documents:upload with your API key. Receive a job_id immediately.

2

Extract

The pipeline auto-detects the document type and runs direct parsing or OCR as appropriate.

3

Classify

The extracted text is scored against your label taxonomy and the best-fit label with confidence is returned.

4

Retrieve

Poll /v1/jobs/{job_id} or receive a signed webhook notification when your results are ready.
